Graphic Design Hardwares

When creating designs on a computer, the results of what you will create depends a lot on what your using and where you are creating your design.
For instance, if you have too much or too little Ghz or when you can't download a photo you want to put in your design, understanding your hardware options will help you get the system you need for the right price.

GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)

This piece of hardware is responsible for processing vast amounts of graphical data in a computer

VRAM,(Video Random Access Memory)

This piece of hardware is used to store memory for a dedicated Graphics card.

Dedicated Graphics Card

a separate hardware component in a computer that enhances visual performance by having its own GPU and dedicated memory.A Dedicated Graphics card relies on its own memory, so it can clear graphics quickly, enabling video editing and complex graphic design.

Peripherals

Peripherals is a piece of hardware that lets you connect to your computer in multiple ways and makes your equipment more adaptable, making it an important component for any travel means.

Processors

Processors/CPUs are an important component of a computer that performs most of the processing inside the computer. A processor has four components:

  • A floating point unit (FPU): This component carries out floating point numbers, such as square root, multiplication, division, subtraction, and addition.
  • An arithmetic logic unit (ALU): What performs the calculations, arithmetic and logic operations.
  • Registers: This component manages the data inside of a computer and tells the ALU what processes must be performed.
  • Cache Memories: This component stores copies of data from the frequently used main locations.
