What are Graphic Designers?

Graphic designers combine art and technology to communicate ideas through images and the layout of websites and printed pages. This often involves creating visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers.
They create a variety of designs to showcase products such as logos, original images, and illustrations.
