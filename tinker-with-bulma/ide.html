What is an IDE?

When two people are working together on the same project, there are two different copies of the project, both people can commit changes to the original project and make changes off of their commits. The two copies are seperated into two softwares known as:

IDE

IDE, also known as an integrated development environment, lets programers test out their code, allowing them to edit and make changes while seeing a preview of it. Works with Github as a Local to make multiple changes to one file.
